Explore Our Tour Places

Deosai National Park

Deosai National Park Tour is an amazing light adventure for families, friends, students, couples and infact everyone.The average height is 4,000m.Also known as the land of Giant, Deosai plains provides the perfect environment for camping.

Hunza Valley

The much renowned Hunza valley is often referred to as heaven on earth, enveloped in the grand Himalayas and the Karakoram mountain ranges, this place has been a great tourist attraction for many years.

Astore Valley

A historical paradise in Pakistan. Astore,a valley 120km long with an area of 5,000km2, The entrance of the valley is located about 60 km southeast of Gilgit with four side valleys. The valley has more than 100 villages. Some of which are Chilm, Bubin, Gorikot, Eid Ghah, Fina, Bulen, Chongra and Tari Shing.

Attabad lake

Attabad Lake has become one of the most popular tourist attractions in Gilgit- Baltistan. Attabad Lake offers many fun-loving activities like jet-skiing, fishing, boating, and some other entertaining activities.Pure water, fresh air, lush alpine flora, snow-capped mountains, green meadows, and mesmerizing views.

K2, Mount Everest

K2,The world’s second highest peak (28,251 feet [8,611 metres]). K2 is located in the Karakoram Range and lies partly in a Chinese-administered enclave of the Kashmir region.The mountain was discovered in 1856.

Ranga ,Balghar

Balghar Ranga is a must see camping and picnic site besides the Shiyok River in Balghar Valley.It is located at around 2 hours drive from Skardu city and offers a nice scenic view of nature and leisure time for families and kids.You will find plenty of herds mostly of horses in them browsing there and this gives the memorable theme and pleasure of Country life.

Upper Kachura Lake, Skardu

Sitting at an altitude of 2,500 m (8,200 ft), Upper Kachura Lake draws visitors to its clear water and mountain vistas. An average summer water temperature of 15C (59F) might make the lake too cold for swimming, but trekking and fishing are prime activities. The lake reaches a depth of 70 m (230 ft).

Serena Shigar Fort

Serena Shigar Fort is a one-of-a-kind heritage boutique hotel situated within a peaceful natural setting. Seek refuge and find adventure in a 400-year-old fort and the 17th century Palace of the Raja of Shigar.Serena Shigar Fort is Graced by the extreme beauty of Baltistan’s Karakoram Mountain Range and four centuries of folklore and legends.

The Cold Desert Sakardu

The Cold Desert, also known as the Katpana Desert or Biama Nakpo,[citation needed] is a high-altitude desert located near Skardu, Gilgit−Baltistan, Pakistan.[1] The desert contains large sand dunes that are sometimes covered in snow during winter. Situated at an elevation of 2,226 metres (7,303 ft) above sea level, the Cold Desert is one of the highest deserts in the world.

Shangrilla Resort,Sakardu

Lower Kachura Lake, also known as Shangrila Lake, is a lake located near the city of Skardu in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. Situated at a height of 2,500 metres (8,200 ft), it has had an operational resort on its bank since 1983.

Chaqchan Mosque Khaplu,Gilgit Baltistan

Initial name of the Chaqchan was "Ju-Khang" that was calls by the Bonpos. This name was changed into 'hLa-khang', when the entire population with their kings embraced into Buddhists faith. During 7th Century this monastery was reconstructed and changed into a Jami-Masjid, when the Noorbakhshi Sufi Muslim priest Mir Syed Ali Hamadani entered into Baltistan and introduced Islam.

Passu Gilgit-Baltistan

Passu is a popular tourist destination in Pakistan and all over the world because of its easily accessible sweeping landscapes, and vistas of the 7,478 m (24,534 ft) tall 'Passu Sar mountain', 'the Passu Glacier', and 'Tupopdan' 6,106m (20,033 ft).
